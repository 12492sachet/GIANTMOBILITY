Affordable Clean Transport Solutions

Giat Mobility is a Kenyan-based startup dedicated to developing affordable, clean transport solutions for the African market.

To provide affordable, sustainable, and eco-friendly transport solutions that empower communities and boost Africa’s economy.

To create cheap, clean, and sustainable transport solutions that empower communities and foster economic growth in Africa.

Our Divisions

Comprehensive mobility solutions for Africa's transportation needs

Giat Bikes

A premier leasing program offering both traditional bikes and e-bikes, designed to provide clients with affordable and efficient mobility solutions.

Giat Engineering

Specializes in developing cutting-edge mobility solutions, including accessories and innovative technologies. We build and maintain charging stations to support clean energy transport systems.

Giat Software

Provides advanced tools for managing operations and external mobility software. Features the Giat Mobility Management Kit, a complete end-to-end platform for leasing, returning, and financial management.

Why choose us

Our unique features and capabilities set us apart, offering innovative solutions tailored to your needs. With a commitment to quality and excellence, we ensure reliability and outstanding results.
